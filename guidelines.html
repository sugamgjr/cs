Community Guidelines

To maintain a safe and welcoming environment for all users, please follow these guidelines when submitting confessions:

Allowed Content

  • Personal experiences and feelings
  • Challenges you're facing
  • Thoughts you're afraid to share elsewhere
  • Questions for the community

Prohibited Content

  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Threats or incitement to violence
  • Personal information about yourself or others
  • Spam or advertisements
  • Illegal content

Moderation

All confessions are reviewed by our moderation team before appearing publicly. We reserve the right to reject or remove any content that violates these guidelines.

By submitting a confession, you agree to these guidelines and our Privacy Policy.
