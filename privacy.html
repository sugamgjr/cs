Privacy Policy

At ConfessionSays, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Don't Collect

We never ask for or store:

  • Your name or contact information
  • Your exact location
  • Your IP address or device information
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Information We Do Collect

The only information we collect is what you voluntarily provide in your confession:

  • The content of your confession
  • Your approximate age (optional)
  • Your gender (optional)
  • Your general location (optional, e.g. "Kathmandu")

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Display your anonymous confession
  • Help us understand our community demographics
  • Improve our service

Data Security

While no online service is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

By using ConfessionSays, you agree to this privacy policy.
